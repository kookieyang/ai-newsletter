Vietnam Becomes First Southeast Asian Nation with Comprehensive AI Law
Vietnam's Law on Artificial Intelligence, which took effect March 1, 2026, is now being cited as a landmark for the region. The law adopts a risk-based approach modeled on the EU AI Act, mandating human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and prohibiting high-risk applications including malicious deepfakes. It applies extraterritorially to foreign AI systems operating in Vietnam. Existing AI systems get 12–18 months to comply. The swift passage has raised compliance concerns among businesses, but positions Vietnam as a regulatory pioneer in Southeast Asia.
