Vietnam Becomes First Southeast Asian Nation with Standalone AI Law
Vietnam's comprehensive Law on Artificial Intelligence, passed in December 2025, took effect on March 1, 2026, making it the first Southeast Asian country with a standalone AI regime. The law adopts a risk-based model similar to the EU AI Act, mandating human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and bans on certain high-risk applications. It has extraterritorial reach, affecting foreign entities operating in Vietnam, and includes grace periods for legacy high-risk systems. South Korea's AI Basic Act also took effect in January 2026, signaling Asia's rapid shift from legislative drafting to real-world enforcement.
