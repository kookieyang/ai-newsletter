Vietnam's AI Law Takes Effect — First Standalone AI Framework in Southeast Asia
Vietnam's comprehensive Law on Artificial Intelligence officially took effect on March 1, making it the first Southeast Asian nation to implement a standalone AI legal framework. Modeled after the EU AI Act's risk-based approach, it mandates human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and bans on certain high-risk applications. The law has extraterritorial application, affecting foreign entities offering AI services in Vietnam. Grace periods of up to 18 months apply for legacy systems in health, education, and finance.
