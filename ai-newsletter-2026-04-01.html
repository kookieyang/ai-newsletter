Vietnam Becomes First Southeast Asian Nation with Standalone AI Law — IP Rules Follow
Vietnam's comprehensive Law on Artificial Intelligence — which took effect March 1 — is now the first standalone AI regime in Southeast Asia. It adopts a risk-based model mandating human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and prohibitions on certain high-risk applications, with extraterritorial reach. As of April 1, updated IP regulations also empower the government to define conditions for establishing intellectual property rights for AI-created works. Grace periods of up to 18 months apply to legacy high-risk systems.
