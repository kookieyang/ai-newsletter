Vietnam's Comprehensive AI Law Takes Effect — First in Southeast Asia
Vietnam became the first Southeast Asian country to implement a comprehensive AI law, effective March 1, 2026. The Law on Artificial Intelligence adopts a risk-based model mandating human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and prohibiting certain high-risk applications. It has extraterritorial application, with grace periods for legacy high-risk systems extending to September 2027. This follows South Korea's AI Basic Act (January 2026) and Taiwan's AI Basic Act (January 2026), marking a wave of Asian AI legislation that now outpaces many Western nations.
