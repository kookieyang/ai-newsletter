Vietnam's Standalone AI Law Takes Effect — First in Southeast Asia
Vietnam became the first Southeast Asian nation with a comprehensive standalone AI law when its Law on Artificial Intelligence took effect on March 1. The risk-based framework mandates human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and bans certain high-risk applications, with extraterritorial reach. The move comes as South Korea's AI Basic Act (effective January 22) and China's revised Cybersecurity Law with AI provisions (January 1) are also being actively enforced. Asia's regulatory landscape is rapidly catching up with the EU.
