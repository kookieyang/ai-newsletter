Vietnam's Comprehensive AI Law Takes Effect — First in Southeast Asia
Vietnam's landmark AI law came into effect on March 1, 2026, becoming the first comprehensive AI legislation in Southeast Asia. Drawing influence from the EU AI Act, it adopts a risk-based model with mandatory human oversight for generative AI, clear labeling requirements for AI-generated content and deepfakes, and extraterritorial application. The government plans to establish a national AI computing center and support development of Vietnamese-language LLMs.
