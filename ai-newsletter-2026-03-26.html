Vietnam Becomes First Southeast Asian Nation with Standalone AI Law
Vietnam's comprehensive Law on Artificial Intelligence, passed in December 2025, officially took effect on March 1, 2026, making it the first Southeast Asian country with a standalone AI legal regime. The law adopts a risk-based model with mandatory human oversight for generative AI, content labeling, and bans on certain high-risk applications. South Korea's AI Basic Act also took effect in January, while Japan's parallel legislation targets high-impact AI areas. Asia-Pacific is now a global leader in AI governance enforcement.
